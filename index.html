Our Lab

NGS Genomics Core Facility Lab

Welcome to our Genomics Core Facility Lab, led by Dr. Bright Adu! We're a dedicated team passionate about unraveling the mysteries encoded in DNA. From decoding genomes to exploring the building blocks of life, our lab is a hub for cutting-edge genomics research. Dr. Adu's leadership guides us as we collaborate, innovate, and make strides in understanding the blueprint of life. Join us on this scientific journey where simplicity meets the complexity of genomics!

Meet the Lab

  

Research Focus

General NGS Sequencing and Bioinformatics Analysis

In our lab, we specialize in the intricate realm of sequencing pathogens and conducting advanced bioinformatics analyses. Through cutting-edge technologies, we unravel the genetic codes of pathogens, enhancing our understanding of their behavior and evolution. Bioinformatics analysis serves as our guiding compass, allowing us to interpret vast genomic data, identify patterns, and derive valuable insights. This symbiotic fusion of sequencing and bioinformatics empowers us to navigate the complex landscape of pathogen genomics, fostering breakthroughs in disease understanding and paving the way for innovative interventions.
